Paris, France


Paris, the capital city of France, is known as the "City of Light" and is famous for its art, culture, fashion, and history. The city offers stunning architecture, world-class museums, and romantic streets that attract millions of tourists every year.

Top 5 Attractions:

  1. Eiffel Tower – Iconic symbol of Paris offering breathtaking views of the city.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to thousands of masterpieces including the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A stunning example of Gothic architecture with historic significance.
  4. Sainte-Chapelle – Famous for its stained glass windows that light up beautifully.
  5. Montmartre – Charming neighborhood known for the Sacré-Cœur Basilica and artistic vibes.

Must-Try Foods:

  • Croissants – Flaky, buttery pastries perfect for breakfast.
  • Baguette – Classic French bread that is crunchy on the outside and soft inside.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes that can be sweet or savory.
  • Macarons – Colorful, delicate sweet treats with a variety of flavors.
  • Escargot – Traditional French dish of snails cooked in garlic butter.
